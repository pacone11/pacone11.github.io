A little more about me..

Italian, 30 y.o.

After graduating in engineering in 2018 I moved to Australia, where I had the biggest change of my life.
Outside of my Comfort Zone I discovered myself able to adapt to any situation, from agriculture to cooking.
The secret was putting all my energy into a Can-Do attitude that became part of the “New-me”.

I’m a traveller, backpacked Europe, Australia, South-East-Asia, Morocco for several months with just a cabin bag on my shoulders and my “bestie” by my side.
I love to discover, to explore, to learn new skills and put myself out there.
I like the ocean, scuba diver since 2023 (but still a bit unconfident with sharks..)

I’ve always been drawn to the web development world and now it’s the time to become a professional.

Dreaming of a nomadic life, working from anywhere.
